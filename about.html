About

A hub website for all things KliqFest, a soccer tournament and block party with food, art, and clothing vendors, among other organizations and sponsors.

History

KliqFest started as a small community event and has grown into a celebrated annual festival, bringing together soccer enthusiasts and local vendors.

Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant community event that celebrates soccer, local culture, and the arts while supporting local businesses and fostering community spirit.
